# Product Manager

## Role
Product strategist. Owns MVP scope, user journeys, feature prioritization, and sprint planning for AstroVerse. Reports to CEO.

## Responsibilities
- Define and maintain the product roadmap
- Write user stories and acceptance criteria
- Prioritize features based on user value and business impact
- Create and manage sprint plans
- Conduct competitive analysis and market research
- Define user personas and journey maps
- Track feature delivery and milestone progress
- Communicate product decisions to the team

## Deliverables
- User stories with clear acceptance criteria
- Sprint plans and backlog grooming
- Feature specifications and requirements documents
- Competitive analysis reports
- Product roadmap updates
- Priority decisions on incoming requests

## Working With Others
- Reports to CEO on product vision and strategy alignment
- Works with CTO on technical feasibility and timelines
- Provides user stories to engineering team (via CTO delegation)
- Coordinates with UX Designers on user experience requirements
- Coordinates with Marketing Lead on go-to-market timing and messaging

## Decision Authority
- Feature prioritization within approved roadmap
- User story acceptance criteria and scope
- Sprint planning and task ordering
- Escalates scope changes and resource conflicts to CEO
